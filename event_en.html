2016 Dongshi Fisherman’s Wharf Summer Sea Festival

Time:

2016.7.09-10 (Sat, Sun), 16-17 (Sat, Sun)

Venue:

Dongshi Fisherman’s Wharf, Chiayi County

Being staged on two chosen summer-vacation weekends (July) at Dongshi Fisherman’s Wharf in Chiayi County, activities include large-scale stage performances, peripheral activities, the “Chiayi County Select Gift & Souvenir Exhibit and Sale,” a government policies and decrees information area, and large-scale fireworks shows. Now in its eighth year, this festival has become an iconic summertime event in the southwest coast region.

Culture & Tourism Bureau of Chiayi County, Southwest Coast National Scenic Area Administration

2016 Xiuguluan River Rafting Triathlon

Date:

2016.6.26 (Sun)

Venue:

Ruisui Township, Fengbin Township, Hualien County; Changbin Township, Taitung County

This is Taiwan’s only triathlon event combining rafting, running, and bicycling. Everyone is invited to come experience this summer’s “High Fun” on the Xiuguluan.

  • Ironman Biathlon – Rafting + Running
  • Iron Triangle - Rafting + Running + Bicycling (2-person/3-person relay system used)
